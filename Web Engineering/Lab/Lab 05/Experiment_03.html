Leading & Tracking Analysis

Typography is the art of arranging text to make written language legible. When line height is too low, text becomes difficult to read as lines overlap vertically, causing eye strain for the user.

Typography is the art of arranging text to make written language legible. By increasing the line height, we create "breathing room" between lines, allowing the eye to track from the end of one line to the start of the next effortlessly.
